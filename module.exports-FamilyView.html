Class: FamilyView

.exports~FamilyView

new FamilyView(jsonopt)

A view of a family that consists of up to two parents and a list of children who have that set of parents in common. While the Relationship data type carries the canonical information about the nature of the relationship between the each pair of persons, the FamilyView is designed as a convenience for display purposes. GEDCOM X RS Spec
